FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our ceramic products, ordering, shipping, care, and more. If you need further information, feel free to contact our support team.

We process orders within 1-2 business days. Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number via email. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the U.S., while international shipping may take 7-14 business days, depending on your location. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your address and order weight.

Our ceramic products are durable but require proper care. Hand-wash your items with mild soap and warm water to preserve their finish. Avoid using abrasive scrubbers, as they may scratch the surface. For decorative pieces, dust them regularly with a soft, dry cloth. Do not expose ceramics to extreme temperature changes to prevent cracking.

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery if the item is unused and in its original packaging. To initiate a return, contact us at support@ceramicshop.com with your order number. We’ll provide a return shipping label, though return shipping costs are the customer’s responsibility unless the item is defective. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

We offer custom orders and personalization for many of our products! You can request specific colors, designs, or engravings through our “Customize” page. Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks to complete, depending on the complexity. A 50% deposit is required upfront, with the remaining balance due upon completion. Contact us for a quote!

Our customer support team is here to help! You can reach us via email at support@ceramicshop.com or by phone at (555) 123-4567, Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM EST. We also have a live chat feature on our website for immediate assistance. For order inquiries, please have your order number ready.
